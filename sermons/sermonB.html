Divine Help Guarantees Rest

Pastor Tolu Otegbade|14 May 2023

The sermon discusses the concept of Divine Help, stating that man is truly at peace when God grants him rest. The speaker highlights that divine help, a crucial need for everyone at some point in life, could involve guidance along a specific path, arriving always at the right time. Divine help is defined as outcomes from various divine interventions and ongoing preservation of our destiny and interests as God's children. The sermon warns that without divine help, one risks being stranded or grounded, and while men can be instruments of God's help, they should not be seen as its source. The most critical form of help needed is deliverance from self.

Notes

  • When God gives man rest, man is truly at peace.

  • One of the biggest needs for man is help. Divine help is a necessity for life. Everyone at a point in their life will need divine help (Hebrews 4:16, and Genesis 2:1-25.)

    • "16 Let us then approach God’s throne of grace with confidence, so that we may receive mercy and find grace to help us in our time of need.”.

  • Sometimes, we need to be patient and willing to follow the Helper as we are guided towards a certain path or destination.

  • God will always come at the right time Exodus 18: 1-27.

    • What is Divine Help?

  • Divine help can be defined as the outcome of several or diverse kinds of divine intervention Acts 21:1-25.

  • Divine help also means minute by minute preservation of our destiny, interests and that which belongs to us as children of God Psalms 121:1-8

    • More notes

  • Divine intervention brings divine help.

  • Anyone that is not divinely helped is potentially stranded and effectively grounded (2 kings 6:1-33,, Psalm 127:1-5).

  • Never look unto man as the source of your help. Men can be instruments used by God..

  • The biggest thing a man needs help from is in the deliverance from self Romans 7:24
