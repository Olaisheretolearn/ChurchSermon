Offering Praise by Faith

Pst. Tolu Otegbade| Heb 11:4 | 04 June 2023

In life, everything is connected and has a foundation. This includes praise, which can be successful or fail depending on how it is given. Effective praise is grounded in a focus on God, who is the foundation it should stand on.

To offer excellent praise, it must be rooted in faith, which is based on love. Love, in turn, is rooted in grace, and grace is rooted in the blood of Jesus. Ultimately, God is the ultimate source and is not rooted in anything. In summary, everything in life is interconnected, and in the Kingdom of God, everything works through faith, love, grace, and God Himself.

Notes

  • Everything in life is rooted or founded in something. Nothing stands alone.

  • Praise does not stand alone. Praise can fail. Its efficacy depends on the kind of praise being offered.

  • Praise can be offered amiss, when it doesn’t have a foundation.

  • When praise does not deliver result, it may not be because it lacks energy or excitement, it is because it’s not an excellent praise. It is without a focus on God which is the Foundation it should stand.

  • When your time of favour comes, even enemies begin to work for you..

  • Every excellent sacrifice of praise must be rooted and grounded in Faith. Heb 11:4

    • 4 By faith Abel brought God a better offering than Cain did. By faith he was commended as righteous, when God spoke well of his offerings. And by faith Abel still speaks, even though he is dead.

  • Remember that in this Kingdom, everything works by Faith.

  • Faith, is rooted in Love. Love is rooted in Grace and Grace is rooted in the Blood. Finally, the Blood is rooted in God. God is not rooted in anything. Gal 5:6

    • Attributes of Praise offered in Faith

  • It is focused on God and God alone. It must be about the person of God and His doings. It must reflect His goodness, glory and His name Heb 13:15 Psa 8:1

  • It has to be basically praiseful. It should not have requests and must only adore God Exodus 15:1-11

  • It must be orderly and decent Psa 150:1-4,Psalm 149:2-3

  • It must be free of monetary cost Matt 10:8
